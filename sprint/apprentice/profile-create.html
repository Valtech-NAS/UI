The Chief Executive of Skills Funding – Candidate terms conditions for use of Apprenticeship vacancies

[This page was last updated on 29 July 2011]

These Terms and Conditions apply to candidates who are registered for the purposes of searching for apprenticeship vacancies on the Apprenticeship vacancies system hereafter called ‘the system’.

These terms and conditions tell you what we will do with the information collected by ‘the system’ and states what we have done to provide a safe website for everyone.

1. Data Protection Act 1998

The Chief Executive of Skills Funding is registered with the Information Commissioner responsible for the Data Protection and Freedom of Information Acts. Our notification can be viewed on the Information Commissioners website at http://www.ico.gov.uk/

You have certain rights over the things you tell us about yourself when using ‘the system’. These rights are given to you under the Data Protection Act 1998.

Personal information from visitors to ‘the system’ is only collected if it is volunteered to us.

‘The system’ is not intended to attract children under the age of 14. The Chief Executive of Skills Funding does not knowingly solicit personal information under the age of 14 or send them requests for personal information. In any event children should always ask their parents, guardians, or a teacher for approval before sending any personal information.

2. Use of personal information

a) ‘The system’

‘The system’ enables individuals to apply for apprenticeships and to be kept informed of apprenticeship opportunities.

‘The system’ also enables Connexions, Jobcentre Plus (JC+), and the Chief Executive of Skills Funding and his staff and organisations contracted by him to deliver the Next Step and the National Careers Service and their employees or agents to search for apprenticeship vacancies and pass details of the vacancy to clients for the purpose of providing careers advice and guidance.

Personal information obtained from ‘the system’ will be provided to learning providers and employers only in order to match registered candidate requirements to possible vacancies for apprenticeships, including those employers or providers offering a guaranteed interview scheme. Learning Providers may act on behalf of their employers to sift and shortlist candidates for interview that meet the criteria set by the employer.

b) In addition information will be used for the following purposes in relation to the running of ‘the system’
• dealing with enquiries about ‘the system’ or responding to a request to the help line
• assisting the registered candidate in the operation and use of ‘the system’
• assisting organisations in supporting the registered candidate in relation to ‘the system’
• notification of changes in how to use ‘the system’
• notification of developments in ‘the system’
• notification by e-shot of vacancies that may be of interest to you

c) The information provided when completing the ‘My areas of interest’ section in the candidates My Profile menu and identifying ‘favourite searches’, will be held securely and only used to help you in future searches and identify by proxy, potential areas of demand by the Chief Executive of Skills Funding. It will not be passed onto any third party.

d) Research and Partner information

Combined statistics about visitors, traffic patterns, answers to questions, content entered, and related site information may be provided to reputable and reliable third parties.

User data will be shared with Partner Organisations, and their employees and agents, in supporting the registered candidate in the searching and application process managed through ‘the system’. Partner Organisations include Connexions, JCP, organisations delivering the Skills the Next Step and All Age Careers Service, Young Peoples Learning Agency, learning providers and their agents involved in providing this service.

Personal information relating to users or administrators of ‘the system’ may be used for administration and research purposes.

e) Additional Uses

The personal information obtained from ‘the system’ (including registration) may be used by the Chief Executive of Skills Funding or by any successors thereof for the purposes described above.

f) Duration of candidate data held and deleting your account

‘The system’ will check for inactive accounts as part of its housekeeping and system performance. Candidates who have completed the registration process but have not logged onto ‘the system’ for 12 months following registration will be deleted. In addition Candidates who have commenced the registration process but have not activated their registration within 30 days of their activation email will also be deleted. In both cases the candidates personal details will be removed, and they will be required to re-register to use ‘the system’.

Candidates also have the ability to remove their details from ‘the system’ in accordance with the Data Protection Act 1998 by deleting their account through their My Profile menu or by contacting the National Helpdesk at nationalhelpdesk@apprenticeships.gov.uk. This will remove all personal details and the account will be deleted. To continue to use ‘the system’ the candidate will need to re-register.

g) Improper use of ‘the system’ and data held within it

The Chief Executive of Skills Funding reserves the right to delete Candidate accounts that make use of inappropriate personal information including email addresses and usernames.

The Chief Executive of Skills Funding shall pass on to third parties personal information relating to members of ‘the system’, including candidates, learning providers, employers or personal information obtained from ‘the system’ for the purpose of investigating any illegal or improper use or behaviour. The Chief Executive of Skills Funding will also disclose your personal information if required to do so by law in urgent circumstances, to protect personal safety, the public or ‘the system’.

3. Security

The Chief Executive of Skills Funding will use reasonable effort to ensure that all personal information relating to ‘the system’ is secure against unauthorised access.

Where the Chief Executive of Skills Funding needs to send the information to third parties for the purpose of providing a service to registered candidates, we will only provide those third parties the information they need to deliver the service, and they are prohibited from using that information for any other purpose.

The Chief Executive of Skills Funding is not responsible for the content or security of any sites linked to and from ‘the system’.

You will keep any passwords or usernames created for the purpose of your use of ‘the system’ confidential and you agree not to disclose your password and username to anyone else.

4. Your Consent

By providing personal information, you consent to the use of this information as described in these Terms and Conditions or as stated at the time that such information is obtained.
These Terms and Conditions may be changed, modified or amended from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page of ‘the system’ so please check it from time to time. Your continued use of ‘the system’ following the posting of any changes to the terms and conditions shall mean that you have accepted those changes.
