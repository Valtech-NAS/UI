1. Vacancy summary

TThe apprenticeship will involve learning to operate a variety of machinery such as milling machines, surface grinders and bandsaws.

The successful candidate will also be required to manufacture parts using raw materials from engineering drawings, sketches or verbal instructions.

Further duties include:

  • checking parts using measuring equipment
  • recording process times
  • reporting defects
  • planning process methods
  • ensuring Health and Safety requirements are adhered to and reporting issues

The apprenticeship is subject to a mandatory 3-month probationary period. There will also be a 1-day trial prior to an offer of the position.

2. Information for candidate

Desired skills

The ideal candidate will have a good command of English, excellent communication skills, a high standard of maths and basic IT knowledge. An interest in mechanical fields would be beneficial.

Reality check

A degree of physical work will be required.

Comfortable with unclean working environments.

You must be capable of standing during all working hours.

Future prospects

Advancement to Level 3 qualification is encouraged and supported.

On successful completion of the apprenticeship, there will be a full-time position available, leading to competitive salary in excess of £28k + benefits.

Long-term potential for career development to a more senior position.

Personal qualities

The candidate must:

  • ensure a confidential approach on sensitive projects
  • be reliable and punctual
  • have an enthusiastic approach to learning
  • be able to work as part of a team and under own initiative

Being a small company we are able to dedicate ourselves to 100% customer focus, delivering high quality parts in short lead-time’s without compromise.
