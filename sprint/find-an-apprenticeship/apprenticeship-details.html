1. Vacancy info

This apprenticeship will have many different aspects to it including, learning to operate a variety of machinery and equipment to include Milling machines, Centre Lathes, Surface Grinders, Universal Grinder, Pillar Drills, Bandsaws, Bench Tools.

The successful candidate will be required to manufacture parts using raw materials such as a variety of Steels, Brass, Bronze, Copper, Aluminium, Plastics and composites from Engineering drawings, sketches or verbal instructions.

The successful candidate will also be:

  • Quality checking parts using appropriate measuring equipment.
  • Recording process times.
  • Reporting defects found.
  • Plan process methods
  • Ensuring work area is maintained to acceptable cleanliness level after use.
  • Ensuring Health and Safety requirements are adhered to and where issues found reported immediately.

2. Employer info

Established in 1978, R G Engineering (Coventry) Ltd is proud to have developed partnerships working with leading companies that include Rolls-Royce Aero Engines, Fanuc Robotics, Denso, Atlas Copco, GKN Aerospace, Pilkington, Lawrence Automotive, Maher, RTC Electronics, Unipart Eberspacher to name just a few. Supplying design and manufacture products that include Prototypes, Gauges, Fixtures, Tooling and onsite customer support to the Aerospace, Automotive, Robotic, Medical, Power Generation and Machine Tool markets.

We are now looking to grow our business by strengthening our team and to pass on over 125 years of combined experience by offering an approved Apprenticeship scheme to a suitable candidate.

Being a small company we are able to dedicate ourselves to 100% customer focus, delivering high quality parts in short lead-time’s without compromise.

4. Course details & requirements

The successful candidate will be trained onsite and be supported by a Valkyrie tutor to achieve the Engineering qualification and functional skills. (where applicable)

Skills desired

The ideal candidate will have good command of English Language, a high standard of maths, basic IT skills and good communication skills.

An interest in mechanical fields would be beneficial.

Reality check

This apprenticeship is subject to a 3 Month probationary period, which is mandatory.

There will be a 1 day minimum Trial prior to offer of position.

Future prospects

Advancement to Level 3 qualification is encouraged and supported.

On successful completion of Apprenticeship, there will be a Full Time position available leading to competitive salary in excess of £28k + benefits.

Long term potential for career development to a more senior position.

Personal qualities

Working on sensitive projects requires confidentiality of projects is essential.

Reliability and punctuality required.

Enthusiastic approach to learning

You will need to work as part of a small team and at times under own initiative.

You must be capable of standing during all working hours.

A degree of physical work will be required.

Comfortable with unclean working environments.

Other information

Free Onsite Car parking available.

Bus service nearby.

Morning Break 10.00 – 10.15 & Lunch 13.00 – 13.30

Overtime during busy periods

Paid annual leave

All Personal Protective Equipment will be provided – Safety Footwear, Safety Glasses, Overalls etc.

Some time available during work hours to complete coursework.

The National Minimum Wage (NMW) for apprentices is £2.68 per hour from 1st October 2013. This applies to 16-18 year old apprentices and those aged over 19 in the first year of their Apprenticeship. For all other apprentices the National Minimum wage appropriate to their age applies.

The Minimum Wage for Apprentices applies to time spent on the job plus time spent training.
