1. Vacancy info

This apprenticeship will have many different aspects to it including, learning to operate a variety of machinery and equipment to include Milling machines, Centre Lathes, Surface Grinders, Universal Grinder, Pillar Drills, Bandsaws, Bench Tools.

The successful candidate will be required to manufacture parts using raw materials such as a variety of Steels, Brass, Bronze, Copper, Aluminium, Plastics and composites from Engineering drawings, sketches or verbal instructions.

The successful candidate will also be:

  • Quality checking parts using appropriate measuring equipment.
  • Recording process times.
  • Reporting defects found.
  • Plan process methods
  • Ensuring work area is maintained to acceptable cleanliness level after use.
  • Ensuring Health and Safety requirements are adhered to and where issues found reported immediately.

2. Employer info

Established in 1978, R G Engineering (Coventry) Ltd is proud to have developed partnerships working with leading companies that include Rolls-Royce Aero Engines, Fanuc Robotics, Denso, Atlas Copco, GKN Aerospace, Pilkington, Lawrence Automotive, Maher, RTC Electronics, Unipart Eberspacher to name just a few. Supplying design and manufacture products that include Prototypes, Gauges, Fixtures, Tooling and onsite customer support to the Aerospace, Automotive, Robotic, Medical, Power Generation and Machine Tool markets.

We are now looking to grow our business by strengthening our team and to pass on over 125 years of combined experience by offering an approved Apprenticeship scheme to a suitable candidate.

Being a small company we are able to dedicate ourselves to 100% customer focus, delivering high quality parts in short lead-time’s without compromise.
