1. Vacancy summary

This Traineeship Opportunity is un-waged Work Experience as part of an entry to work course.

Work Experience Purpose:

To gain experience in the provision of effective administrative support to a variety of teams across the Professional Services Area within Centro (Legal, Finance, HR, Procurement), ensuring high levels of standards are delivered through the provision of effective systems, procedures and support services.

Tasks involved:

  • Undertake office and systems administration.
  • Liaise with relevant internal stakeholders.
  • Ensure the proactive co-ordination of diaries and meetings.
  • Arrange meeting rooms and refreshments as required.
  • Ensure office supplies are monitored and replenished as required.
  • Open and distribute post throughout the Directorate.
  • Maintain and develop archive and filing systems.
  • Maintain electronic database systems.
  • Provide general administrative/office support.
  • Work flexibly to the needs and requirements of Centro.
  • To ensure the security and confidentiality of all files and business matters.

This traineeship work experience is part of an education and training programme that is focused on giving young people the skills and experience that employers are looking for. At its core is work preparation training, English and maths for those who need it, and a high quality work experience placement.

2. Information for candidate

Desired skills & qualities

  • Good Communication and inter-personal skills.
  • Good organisation
  • Diplomacy and confidentiality is essential
  • Positive attitude
  • Eager to learn and develop new skills and knowledge

Other information

Travel expenses will be paid for the duration of the programme.

Future prospects

After successful completion of the Traineeship course, you will receive a certificate of completion and an f reference as a minimum, to support future job applications.

There may also be the possible progression onto an apprenticeship within the company.

Are you eligible?

  1. Are you currently not in a job and have little or no relevant Work Experience
  2. Are you aged 16-18 and qualified below Level 3 (A level) or 19 to 23 and not yet achieved a Full Level 2 (5 GCSE’s at grade C or above inclusive of Math and English)
  3. Think you could be ready for employment or an apprenticeship within 6 months
