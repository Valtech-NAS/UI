1. Summary of role

This traineeship is unpaid work experience as part of an entry to work course.

Role purpose:

To gain experience in the provision of effective administrative support to a variety of teams across the Professional Services Area within Centro (Legal, Finance, HR, Procurement).

Main tasks:

  • Undertake office and systems administration.
  • Liaise with relevant internal stakeholders.
  • Ensure the proactive co-ordination of diaries and meetings.

  • Working week

  • Maximum 30 hours Mon - Fri

  • Expected duration

  • Up to 6 months
  • Distance
  • 0.7 miles
  • Reference number
  • VAC000409315

Content provided by Centro - 20 May 14

2. Are you eligible to apply?

Anyone can apply for this role if they're:

  1. eligible to work in England
  2. unemployed and have little or no work experience
  3. aged 16-18 and qualified below Level 3 or aged 19-23 and qualified below Level 2

3. Information for candidate

Desired skills & qualities

  • Good communication and personal skills.
  • Ability to organise
  • Eager to develop new skills and knowledge

Other information

Travel expenses will be paid for the duration of the programme.

Future prospects

After successful completion of the Traineeship course, you will receive a certificate of completion and a reference to support future job applications. There may also be the possible progression onto an apprenticeship within the company.
