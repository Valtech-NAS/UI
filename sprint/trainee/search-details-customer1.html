1. Opportunity summary

This Traineeship Opportunity, is unwaged Work Experience as part of an entry to work course

Tiverton Pool & Fitness Centre and Lifetime Training are looking for enthusiastic and friendly 16-23 year olds, who want to break into the fitness industry, to join them on a Traineeship. Traineeships are a new government programme designed to give young people the skills and experience needed to secure a job or apprenticeship.

The Lifetime Training Fitness Traineeship contains three core elements:

  • Work preparation training (CV writing and interview techniques)
  • Work placement (at Tiverton Pool & Fitness Centre) where you’ll gain Industry knowledge and skills specific to the fitness industry

It’s a great opportunity to get your foot in the door, learn some great skills to help spruce up your CV and impress an employer.

As part of this traineeship you will be working in all areas of the gym, including gaining experience with customers in reception.

Apply now, to find out more about working in one of the most exciting industries.

This traineeship work experience is part of an education and training programme that is focused on giving young people the skills and experience that employers are looking for. At its core is work preparation training, English and maths for those who need it, and a high quality work experience placement.

2. Information for candidate

Desired skills & qualities

You need to have a keen interest in the health and fitness industry.

You must be enthusiastic and able to work well in a team.

Other information

Consisting of a 12 week programme, the first week is totally guided learning with one of our tutors (Monday –Friday) weeks 2-7 will consist of the student attending the placement work place 3 days a week full time and an additional 2 days a week will be back in the classroom with the tutor, weeks 8-12 will the totally full time in the placement supported with a visit from a lifetime tutor.

Future prospects

Gain the skills and experience needed to secure a job or apprenticeship.

We will help you with this by providing you with an exit interview, as well as a letter of reference and a certificate of completion.

Are you eligible?

  1. Are you currently not in a job and have little or no relevant Work Experience
  2. Are you aged 16-18 and qualified below Level 3 (A level) or 19 to 23 and not yet achieved a Full Level 2 (5 GCSE’s at grade C or above inclusive of Math and English)
  3. Think you could be ready for employment or an apprenticeship within 6 months
