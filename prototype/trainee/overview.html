Find a traineeship

You can apply for a traineeship if you’re:

  • eligible to work in England
  • unemployed and have little or no work experience
  • aged 16 to 18 and qualified below Level 3, or aged 19 to 24 and qualified below Level 2
You may be ready for an apprenticeship if you already have some work experience.
Start now on apprenticeship vacancy matching service

Before you start

A traineeship is a course to get you ready for work or an apprenticeship. It can take up to 6 months.

You’ll get:

  • help with English and maths (if you need it)
  • a work experience placement lasting at least 6 weeks

You won’t be paid, but your employers can sometimes give you expenses for things like travel and meals.
